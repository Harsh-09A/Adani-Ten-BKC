Welcome To Adani Ten BKC

A fabulous residential address in the heart of Bandra East, Ten BKC by Adani Realty brings a posh world of business & leisure to your feet. Its lavish 3 & 4 BHK apartments allow you to come home to plush, cosy interiors & beautiful views of the city. World class amenities such as a swimming pool, game simulator, fitness centre, and salon & spa add to your luxury living. 5 levels of basement parking further bring convenience, while 2.9 acres of open greens bring a peaceful atmosphere.

Surrounding Ten BKC is the finest collection of landmarks, schools, restaurants, & hospitals with MIG Cricket Club, Jio World Drive, & S. L. Raheja Hospital in the vicinity. The area is also well-known for its quick connectivity, being near the Western Express Highway, Bandra-Worli Link Road, Eastern Express Highway, & Bandra Railway Station. In fact, the Mumbai International Airport is within 20 minutes away! In short - Ten BKC affords a lovely lifestyle in one of the most luxe parts of the city, where the beautiful evening-lit views of the city are always a pleasure to behold!.
