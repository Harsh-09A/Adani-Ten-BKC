Welcome To Adani Ten BKC

A fabulous residential address in the heart of Bandra East, Ten BKC by Adani Realty brings a posh world of business & leisure to your feet. Its lavish 3 & 4 BHK apartments allow you to come home to plush, cosy interiors & beautiful views of the city.

Surrounding Ten BKC is the finest collection of landmarks, schools, restaurants, & hospitals with MIG Cricket Club, Jio World Drive, & S. L. Raheja Hospital in the vicinity. The area is also well-known for its quick connectivity, being near the Western Express Highway, Bandra-Worli Link Road, Eastern Express Highway, & Bandra Railway Station.
